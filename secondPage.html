Bike Safety. Even though people bike for leisure and to have fun, they must also consider the consequences of not wearing proper equipment such as a helmet and kneepads. Safety should always be the first step people think of before pushing their foot down on that pedal. If you'd like to know more about bike safety and proper equipment, feel free to join us for our bicycle seminars! They will be held once a month to inform those who are interested in learning and to create a safer environment for everyone.

Cycling Trips

Interested in mountain biking and want challenging terrain? We have an adventure waiting for you! We have the perfect location for you in our cycling trips with rocks, roots, and declining slopes that make your heart beat rapidly. Whistler in British Columbia offers this landscape that intrigues mountain bikers from all around. It is infamous for its downhill descents and puts your skills to the test. We also have other trails that are less scary like Backbone Trail in California giving beginners a taste of the journey that begins with slopes that are easier to perfect.

Do you find these heights dreadful and life threatening? No worries! We have the perfect solution for you. We offer leisure biking in various destinations that provide you with scenery, entertainment, staying fit, as well as bonding time with your family. We have a variety for you to choose from to enjoy your experience with us!

Our locations for leisure biking include Copenhagen, which is Denmark's city capital. This unique area is notorious for bikers and includes more than 180 miles for bike paths and lanes. Then, theres Maple Grove in Brooklyn Park. It does not disappoint with its' 19 miles of trail and 6 mile trail that leads to Elm Creek to the Coon Rapids Dam Regional Park on the Mississippi River.
